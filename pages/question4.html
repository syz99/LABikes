Question 4 - L.A. Bikeshare Metro's Regular Commuters

51,123
Regular Commuters!


This pie chart gives a simple visual into passholder type frequencies. Note there is one point that has been excluded during data cleaning simply because it contained many null values. Overall, to answer this question, we make the assumption that "regular commuters" are defined as those with Monthly and Annual/Flex passes. Thus, we simply summed the frequency of the two passes to get a total of 51,123 regular commuters in the data set.
