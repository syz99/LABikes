Question 2 - Popularity of Start/Stop Stations

* Best to view visuals in Full Screen mode (click 'Full Screen' in bottom right of modules)

From our analysis, starting station 3069 is most popular with 5,138 visits in the range of the data set (July 7, 2016 - April 4, 2017) followed relatively closely by stations 3030, 3005, 3064, and 3031 with a range of 4,629 to 5,059 visits.



From our analysis, ending station 3005 is most popular with 6,262 visits in the range of the data set (July 7, 2016 - April 4, 2017) followed by stations 3031, 3014, 3042, and 3069 with a range of 5,072 to 5,517 visits.

Overall, it seems station is one of the most popular stations as it ranks second in starting and first in ending visits. Station 3005 could be a station to targeted heavily for walk-up usage as this popularity could indicate a heavy tourist area. This station could also be thought of as a great place for advertisement space if the LA bikeshare metro sought additional advertisement revenue.
